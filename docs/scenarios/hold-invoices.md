Title: HOLD invoices
Description: Create an invoice that can be conditionally settled or cancelled.
Education: Hold invoices allow you to accept a payment but delay the final settlement. This is useful for escrow-like scenarios where you want to verify something before completing the payment. The invoice must be settled before the specified block time.
Complexity: Medium
